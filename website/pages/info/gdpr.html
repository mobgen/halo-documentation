HALO Data Protection

Last update at 4 May 2018

In order to use HALO CMS, you need a user profile. We don't collect any other personal data for other purposes than creation of a user. For this we use your email and name. We log IP addresses to trace security attacks or non-authorised access to user accounts.

We process your personal data in accordance with the applicable Accenture Policy (https://www.accenture.com/gb-en/privacy-policy) and may transfer personal data to our contracted service providers who may be located in other countries (outside EEA).

Please state that you have read and understood what we do with your information. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to your project manager.
